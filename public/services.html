Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I track my shipment?
Simply enter your tracking code on our homepage or tracking page. You'll receive real-time updates on your shipment's location, status, and estimated delivery time. You can also sign up for email or SMS notifications.
How long does delivery take?
Delivery times vary by service type: Express Delivery: 1-2 business days, Standard: 3-5 business days, Air Freight: 2-5 business days internationally, Ocean Freight: 15-30 days, Trucking: 1-5 business days domestically.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes! NexShip Xpress ships to over 200 countries worldwide. We offer both air and ocean freight options for international shipments, with full customs clearance support and documentation assistance.
How do I get a shipping quote?
You can get a quote by contacting our customer service team at nexshipxpress@outlook.com or via WhatsApp. For a personalized quote based on your specific shipping needs, visit our contact page.
What items are prohibited from shipping?
Hazardous materials, flammable liquids, explosives, illegal items, firearms, and perishable goods without proper packaging are prohibited. Contact us for a complete list of restricted items and special handling requirements.
