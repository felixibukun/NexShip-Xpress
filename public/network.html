Frequently Asked Questions

About our global network

Which countries do you ship to?
NexShip Xpress ships to over 200 countries worldwide. We have major hubs in North America, Europe, Asia Pacific, South America, and Africa. If you need delivery to a specific country, please contact our customer service team for availability and estimated delivery times.
How long does international shipping take?
International shipping times vary by destination and service type. Express air freight: 2-5 business days, Standard air freight: 5-7 business days, Ocean freight: 15-30 days depending on origin and destination. We provide real-time tracking for all international shipments.
Do you offer customs clearance assistance?
Yes! We provide full customs clearance assistance for all international shipments. Our team of experienced customs brokers will handle all documentation, duties, and taxes to ensure smooth clearance through customs. We also offer DDP (Delivered Duty Paid) services for select destinations.
What is the largest package you can ship?
We can ship packages of virtually any size. For express delivery, we accept packages up to 150 lbs. For freight shipments (air, ocean, trucking), we can handle oversized and heavy cargo including industrial equipment, vehicles, and project cargo. Contact us for a customized solution for your specific requirements.
