Frequently Asked Questions

How long does ocean freight take?
Ocean freight typically takes 15-30 days depending on the origin and destination. Transatlantic shipments average 10-14 days, while transpacific shipments average 15-20 days. We provide real-time vessel tracking so you always know where your cargo is.
What is the difference between FCL and LCL?
FCL (Full Container Load) is when you book an entire container exclusively for your cargo. LCL (Less than Container Load) is when you share container space with other shippers, making it ideal for smaller shipments that don't require a full container.
Do you handle customs clearance?
Yes, we provide full customs clearance services worldwide. Our team of licensed customs brokers handles all documentation, duties, and taxes on your behalf to ensure smooth port clearance at both origin and destination.
