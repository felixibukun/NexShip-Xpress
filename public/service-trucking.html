Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between FTL and LTL?
FTL (Full Truckload) is when your shipment fills an entire truck exclusively for your cargo. LTL (Less than Truckload) is when your shipment shares truck space with other shipments, making it ideal for smaller loads that don't require a full truck.
Do you offer expedited shipping?
Yes, we offer expedited trucking services for time-sensitive shipments. Our expedited service provides dedicated vehicles and direct routing for the fastest possible delivery. Contact us for availability and pricing in your area.
Can you handle hazardous materials?
Yes, we are fully certified to transport hazardous materials. Our drivers are HAZMAT-certified and our vehicles are equipped with specialized safety equipment. Proper documentation and packaging are required. Contact us for specific requirements.
