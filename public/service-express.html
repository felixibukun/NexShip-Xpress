Frequently Asked Questions

About Express Delivery

What is the cutoff time for same-day delivery?
Same-day delivery cutoff is 12:00 PM local time for most areas. Orders placed after cutoff will be delivered the next business day. Contact us for specific cutoff times in your area.
Do you deliver on weekends?
Yes! We offer Saturday delivery for an additional fee. Sunday delivery is available in select major metropolitan areas. Please contact us to check availability in your area.
What happens if my package is delayed?
We take delays seriously. If your package is delayed beyond our guaranteed delivery window, you may be eligible for a shipping refund. Contact our customer service team for assistance with any delayed shipments.
