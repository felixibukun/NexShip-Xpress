Frequently Asked Questions

How long does air freight take?
Air freight typically takes 2-5 business days for international shipments, depending on the origin and destination. Express options are available for urgent deliveries.
What documentation is required?
We provide full documentation assistance including air waybills, commercial invoices, packing lists, and customs declarations. Our team handles all paperwork for you.
Can you ship hazardous materials?
Yes, we are certified to handle dangerous goods and hazardous materials. Special packaging and documentation are required. Contact us for specific requirements.
